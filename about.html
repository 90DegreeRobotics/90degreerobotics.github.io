About 90° ROBOTICS

Building the future of consumer robotics through innovation, accessibility, and thoughtful design.

Our Mission

90° ROBOTICS exists to create advanced robotics and pioneering design techniques that push the boundaries of modern technology. We believe that robotics should be accessible, practical, and transformative—helping to enhance everyday life while maintaining affordability and ease of use.

Our Vision

We envision a future where robotics is seamlessly integrated into daily life. Our aim is to create intelligent systems that amplify human potential, support independence, and drive community progress. This means creating robots that are not only smart—but empathetic, adaptive, and approachable.

Development Framework: Phase 1

Our foundational phase focuses on creating impactful products that showcase our core values of innovation, usability, and ethical design.

  • WagonBot: WagonBot is currently in development as a powered, self-balancing utility wagon designed to carry loads of up to three hundred pounds. Engineered for personal and professional use, it’s built to handle a variety of terrains while maintaining stability and ease of control. While not yet available for purchase, WagonBot represents our commitment to building practical, user-friendly robotics that can truly assist in everyday tasks.
  • Smart Cart: A conceptual design, Smart Cart is envisioned as an autonomous, vision-assisted shopping companion. It will feature real-time item recognition, inventory tracking, and adaptive navigation to assist users in retail or personal environments. This project remains in the ideation stage, forming a core part of our long-term roadmap.
  • SR1 (Small Robot 1): SR1 is a compact mobile robot designed for early-stage testing and development. Capable of object recognition, person or pet following, and remote control, it also serves as a proving ground for LLM-based interaction. SR1’s modular framework allows continuous upgrades in both software and hardware.
  • AURA (Autonomous User Robotic Assistant): AURA is a humanoid home assistant envisioned to support users with daily tech tasks, smart home setup, and general troubleshooting. It will utilize advanced sensors, facial recognition, and a conversational AI to deliver contextual help, security monitoring, and adaptive behavior.

Future Roadmap
