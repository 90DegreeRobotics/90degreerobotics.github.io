ABOUT US

Learn more about 90° ROBOTICS and our mission to revolutionize consumer robotics.

OUR MISSION

90° ROBOTICS exists to create advanced robotics and pioneering design techniques that push the boundaries of modern technology.

We believe that consumer-grade robotics should be accessible, practical, and transformative. Our team is dedicated to developing innovative solutions that enhance everyday life while maintaining affordability and ease of use.

By combining cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design, we aim to create products that seamlessly integrate into your daily routines and provide meaningful value.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where advanced robotics technology is an integral part of everyday life, enhancing human capabilities and improving quality of life.

At 90° ROBOTICS, we're working toward a world where:

  • Robotics technology is accessible to everyone, not just industrial applications
  • Consumer products incorporate intelligent automation to save time and effort
  • Innovative solutions address real-world challenges in homes and communities
  • Technology enhances human capabilities rather than replacing human interaction

DEVELOPMENT FRAMEWORK

Phase 1: Foundational Features

Our initial phase focuses on developing core products that demonstrate our capabilities and establish our presence in the consumer robotics market.

Key Projects:

  • Team Mobile Wagon: Self-balancing utility wagon for lawn mowing and personal use
  • Deep Dropping Cart: Vision-assisted, self-navigating cart for retail automation

Phase 2: Proprietary AI Platform

Our second phase involves developing our proprietary AI platform that will power all our future products and enable advanced capabilities.

Key Developments:

  • Machine learning models for adaptive behavior
  • Computer vision systems for environmental awareness
  • Decision-making algorithms for autonomous operation
  • Integration framework for existing and future products

Future Roadmap

As we continue to grow and develop our technology, we plan to expand our product line and capabilities to address a wider range of consumer needs.

Near-Term Goals:

  • Refine and enhance our existing products
  • Establish partnerships with retailers and distributors
  • Develop community support and feedback channels

Long-Term Vision:

  • Expand into additional consumer robotics categories
  • Develop integrated ecosystem of smart home robotics
  • Establish 90° ROBOTICS as a leader in consumer automation
