OUR MISSION

90 Degree Robotics exists to invent advanced robotics and pioneering design techniques that drive U.S.-based manufacturing and push the boundaries of modern technology.

We are committed to developing innovative consumer-grade robotics solutions that enhance everyday life while maintaining affordability and ease of use.

OUR VISION

We envision a world where smart robotic devices are an everyday part of life — designed, built, and refined in the United States. 90 Degree Robotics aims to help lead that charge, positioning small, agile teams at the forefront of a robotics renaissance.

The power of small teams lies in their ability to collaborate closely, iterate rapidly, and move without the drag of corporate bureaucracy. This speed and flexibility fuels true innovation — and it's how we intend to stay ahead.
