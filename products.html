TEAM MOBILE WAGON

A self-balancing utility wagon designed for lawn mowing and personal use.

The Team Mobile Wagon represents our commitment to creating practical robotics solutions for everyday tasks. This versatile platform combines self-balancing technology with robust utility features to assist with lawn care and other outdoor activities.

Key Features:

  • Self-balancing technology for stability on various terrains
  • Modular accessory system for different applications
  • Intelligent load distribution and weight management
  • Weather-resistant construction for outdoor durability
  • User-friendly controls and intuitive operation

Applications:

  • Lawn care equipment transport
  • Garden maintenance assistance
  • Outdoor event support
  • Personal item transportation

DEEP DROPPING CART

A vision-assisted, self-navigating cart designed for retail automation.

The Deep Dropping Cart revolutionizes the retail experience by combining computer vision with autonomous navigation. This innovative solution helps streamline operations in retail environments while enhancing the customer experience.

Key Features:

  • Vision-assisted navigation for obstacle avoidance
  • Self-navigating capabilities in structured environments
  • Intelligent item recognition and inventory tracking
  • Customizable interface for different retail applications
  • Energy-efficient operation for all-day use

Applications:

  • Retail store assistance
  • Inventory management
  • Customer service enhancement
  • Automated checkout support
