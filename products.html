OUR PRODUCTS & PLATFORMS

Explore our robotics hardware, software, and educational initiatives.

WagonBot

A powered, self-balancing utility wagon in development, designed for heavy loads and versatile terrains.

WagonBot is engineered for both personal and professional use, capable of carrying loads up to three hundred pounds. It aims to provide stability and ease of control across various environments, representing our commitment to practical, user-friendly robotics for everyday tasks. Currently in development and not yet available for purchase.

Key Features (Planned/In Development):

  • Self-balancing technology for stability
  • Powered drive system for effortless hauling
  • 300 lbs load capacity
  • All-terrain capability
  • User-friendly controls and intuitive operation
  • Modular accessory potential

Potential Applications:

  • Lawn care and landscaping equipment transport
  • Garden maintenance and material hauling
  • Outdoor event support and logistics
  • Personal item and gear transportation
  • Workshop and job site assistance

Smart Cart (Conceptual)

An envisioned autonomous, vision-assisted shopping companion for retail and personal use.

Smart Cart is a conceptual design forming a core part of our long-term roadmap. It's envisioned to feature real-time item recognition, inventory tracking, and adaptive navigation to streamline shopping experiences and assist users in various environments. This project is currently in the ideation stage.

Key Features (Envisioned):

  • Vision-assisted navigation for obstacle avoidance
  • Autonomous operation in structured environments
  • Intelligent item recognition and categorization
  • Real-time inventory tracking and list management
  • Customizable interface for diverse applications
  • Energy-efficient design for extended use

Potential Applications:

  • Enhanced retail store shopping assistance
  • Automated inventory management for businesses
  • Personalized shopping experiences
  • Support for assisted living or accessibility needs

AURA (Conceptual)

An Autonomous User Robotic Assistant envisioned as a versatile humanoid helper for home environments.

AURA represents our long-term vision for a personal robotic assistant. Designed to be approachable and helpful, AURA aims to support users with daily tech tasks, manage smart home devices, provide security monitoring, and offer general assistance through natural interaction. This concept focuses on integrating advanced AI, sensing, and mobility into a user-friendly form factor.

Potential Features:

  • Advanced sensor integration (vision, audio, environmental)
  • Facial recognition and user personalization
  • Conversational AI for natural language interaction
  • Contextual task assistance and automation
  • Smart home device control and integration
  • Security monitoring and alert capabilities
  • Adaptive learning based on user interaction

90° Core AI

The intelligent software platform powering our robots and enabling seamless home automation integration.

The 90° Core AI is the brain behind our robotics ecosystem. It's being developed as a custom AI platform focused on secure, efficient control of both our robotic hardware (like WagonBot and future concepts like AURA) and compatible smart home devices. This platform prioritizes user privacy, intuitive interaction, and adaptive learning to create a truly integrated and helpful smart environment.

Key Capabilities (In Development):

  • Unified control interface for robots and smart home devices
  • Secure, encrypted communication protocols
  • Adaptive learning for personalized automation routines
  • Natural language processing for voice and text commands
  • Cross-device compatibility and interoperability
  • On-device processing potential for enhanced privacy
  • Developer APIs for future expansion

SR1 (Small Robot 1)

A compact mobile robot designed for early-stage testing, development, and AI interaction research.

SR1 serves as a versatile and foundational platform for 90° ROBOTICS. It's instrumental in testing core functionalities like object recognition, person/pet following, and remote control systems. Furthermore, SR1 is a key proving ground for integrating and refining LLM-based interactions, allowing for continuous upgrades in both its software intelligence and hardware capabilities thanks to its modular framework.

Key Aspects & Uses:

  • Development platform for computer vision algorithms
  • Testbed for autonomous navigation and obstacle avoidance
  • Prototyping human-robot interaction (HRI) with LLMs
  • Modular design for easy hardware/software upgrades
  • Remote teleoperation and control system testing
  • Foundation for more complex robotic systems

Educational Robotics Kit (Conceptual)

A modular robotics platform concept designed to inspire and teach programming, engineering, and problem-solving.

We envision an Educational Robotics Kit that empowers learners of all ages. This kit would provide hands-on experience with building and programming robots, fostering an understanding of core STEM concepts. The focus would be on modularity, allowing for progressive learning and creative exploration, from basic movements to complex sensor-based behaviors.

Potential Features & Goals:

  • Programmable microcontroller (e.g., Raspberry Pi, Arduino compatible)
  • Diverse range of sensors (e.g., ultrasonic, line-following, light)
  • Various actuators (e.g., motors, servos)
  • Easy-to-assemble mechanical components
  • Project-based learning modules and curriculum support
  • Scalable for different skill levels (beginner to advanced)
  • Community platform for sharing projects and ideas
